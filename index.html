What are Chao?

Chao are little creatures with a big drop-like shaped head and big oval eyes, a small round body with wings, and small limbs and tail. Chao also have an floating spherical ball above its head which it uses to show its emotions and mood. In Sonic Earth's Chao Garden you get a chance to take care of these adorable creatures. This wiki is here to help guide you. This small creatures are suprisingly complex.
